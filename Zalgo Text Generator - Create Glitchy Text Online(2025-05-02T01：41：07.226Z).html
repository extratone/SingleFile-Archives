The Origin Of Zalgo

Zalgo is the creation of a Flash animator known as Dave Kelly. The word Zalgo was often mentioned in Archies or Garfield comic strips from the newspaper that used to denote something scary or full of terror. It is also used to address a being who is often called as he who waits behind the wall. The main purpose behind bringing the word Zalgo was to represent the dark and demonic things with it.
